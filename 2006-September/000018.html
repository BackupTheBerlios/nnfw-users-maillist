Yes Edu,
you understood me even better then myself.
In fact my proposal was to call 'OutputFunction' what now is 
'ClusterUpdater' and 'TransferFunction' what now is lacking, that is a 
funciton used by Linkers for writing on the Clusters' inputs. But since 
in systems theory the TransferFunction of a system (in our case the 
neuron) is what does the whole input-output process, then using this 
name for a sub-part of the process would be misleading.
So, agree that the name of the (eventual) new class to be used by Linker 
should be somehting like 'InputFunction' or, even better, 
'NetInputFunction'.
And now... back to work.
