Hello,
I'm working on ART networks. But because I'm a bit busy... the
development goes on very slowly :-(
However... in the last version 1.1.3... I added some classes for an easy
setup of ART networks, in particular:
 - competitive activation on cluster
 - the "log like" output function used in ART's neurons
 - the "gain" output function used for add gain to neurons
But there are still missing some important features in order to get a
complete ART network:
 - inibition of neurons
 - and conditional step-loops

I'll post a news on the forum (http://www.nnfw.org/forum ) when the ART
network will be finished.
